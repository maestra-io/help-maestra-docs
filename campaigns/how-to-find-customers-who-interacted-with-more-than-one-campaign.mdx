# **How to find customers**

Go to **Customer Data Platform→ Customers**.

![](/images/imported/How-to-find-customers-who-interacted-with-a-campaign-(2).png)

## Searching by campaign status

In the **Campaigns** filter group, select the status you need.

![](/images/imported/--2026-01-08--15_42_25.png)

- Find customers who have been sent a campaign at least once.

![](/images/imported/How-to-find-customers-who-interacted-with-campaigns.png)

- Find customers who have never been sent a campaign.

![](/images/imported/How-to-find-customers-who-interacted-with-campaigns-(1).png)

- Find customers who have at least one campaign send failure.

![](/images/imported/How-to-find-customers-who-interacted-with-campaigns-(2).png)

- Find customers who have at least one undelivered campaign.

![](/images/imported/How-to-find-customers-who-interacted-with-campaigns-(3).png)

- Find customers who have opened any campaign at least once.

![](/images/imported/How-to-find-customers-who-interacted-with-campaigns--284-29.png)

- Find customers who have never opened the campaigns sent to them. ​**Note:** Make sure to include **campaign sends** in the filter. Otherwise, the filter will also return customers who were never on any campaign recipient list, since they have no open events.
  ![](/images/imported/How-to-find-customers-who-interacted-with-campaigns-(5).png)
- Find customers who have clicked any link in a campaign at least once.
  ![](/images/imported/How-to-find-customers-who-interacted-with-campaigns-(6).png)
- Find customers who have clicked an unsubscribe link at least once.
  ![](/images/imported/How-to-find-customers-who-interacted-with-campaigns-(7).png)
- Find customers who have participated in a campaign at least once (were on the recipient list). 
  ![](/images/imported/--2026-01-08--15_39_58.png)

## Searching by campaign group: channel, type, folder, topic, tags
Each **status filter** has a nested **Campaigns** condition, where you can specify which [type of campaigns](https://help.maestra.io/en/articles/11897760-types-of-campaigns-available-in-maestra) to search for.
Available filtering options:
- Campaign type (automated, bulk, transactional)
- Topics
- Folders
- Channels (Email, SMS, MobilePush, WebPush, WhatsApp)
- Tags
- Brands
  ![](/images/imported/How-to-find-customers-who-interacted-with-campaigns-(10).png)
- Find customers have been sent mobile push campaigns.
  ![](/images/imported/How-to-find-customers-who-interacted-with-campaigns-(11).png)
- Find customers who opened automated campaigns.
  ![](/images/imported/How-to-find-customers-who-interacted-with-campaigns-(12).png)
- Find customers who clicked campaigns from a specific folder.
  ![](/images/imported/How-to-find-customers-who-interacted-with-campaigns-(13).png)
- Find customers who failed to receive a campaign with a specific tag (send or delivery failed).
  ![](/images/imported/How-to-find-customers-who-interacted-with-campaigns-(14).png)

## Searching by interaction time

- Find customers who were sent campaigns in the first half of 2024.
  ![](/images/imported/How-to-find-customers-who-interacted-with-campaigns-(15).png)
- Find customers who were sent campaigns within the last six months.
  ![](/images/imported/How-to-find-customers-who-interacted-with-campaigns-(16).png)
- Find customers who have clicked on automated email campaigns within the last hour.
  ![](/images/imported/How-to-find-customers-who-interacted-with-campaigns-(17).png)

## Searching by number of interactions

- Find customers who were sent four or more web pushes in the last month. 
  ![](/images/imported/How-to-find-customers-who-interacted-with-campaigns-(18).png)

# How to find specific Campaign engagements (e.g. clicked, sent, opened etc.)
Go to **Customer Data Platform → Campaign engagements**.

  ![](/images/imported/How-to-find-customers-who-interacted-with-a-campaign-(20).png)
- Find all campaign opens.
  ![](/images/imported/--2026-01-15--17_23_56.png)
- Find all clicks on automated web pushes from a folder.
  ![](/images/imported/--2026-01-15--17_26_06.png)
- Find all undelivered SMS in the last 15 minutes.
  ![](/images/imported/--2026-01-15--17_27_36.png)
