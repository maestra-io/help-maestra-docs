Let’s take a look at how customers interact with a campaign.

# How to find customers

Go to **Customer Data Platform→ Customers**.

![](/images/imported/How-to-find-customers-who-interacted-with-a-campaign-(2).png)

We’ll use a "Welcome / Referral program" campaign as an example.

## Searching by campaign status

In the **Campaigns** filter group, select the status you need.
​

![](/images/imported/--2026-01-08--15_42_25.png)

Each status has a nested filter — **Campaign **— pick the one you need.

![](/images/imported/How-to-find-customers-who-interacted-with-a-campaign-(4).png)

- Find customers who were sent the campaign.

![](/images/imported/How-to-find-customers-who-interacted-with-a-campaign-(5).png)

- Find customers who were unable to send the campaign.

![](/images/imported/How-to-find-customers-who-interacted-with-a-campaign-(7).png)

- Find customers who were unable to deliver the campaign. 

![](/images/imported/How-to-find-customers-who-interacted-with-a-campaign--286-29.png)

- Find customers who were in the campaign recipient list but could not receive it due to a send or delivery failure.

![](/images/imported/How-to-find-customers-who-interacted-with-a-campaign-(8).png)

- Find customers who received the campaign.

There is no separate "Delivered" status. To find these customers, set the following two conditions: "The campaign was sent" and "There were no campaign delivery failures."

![](/images/imported/How-to-find-customers-who-interacted-with-a-campaign-(9).png)

- Find customers who opened the campaign. 

![](/images/imported/How-to-find-customers-who-interacted-with-a-campaign-(10).png)

- Find customers who didn’t open the campaign.

**Note:** Make sure to include **campaign sends** in the filter. Otherwise, customers who weren’t included in the recipient list may appear as well, since they have no opens for the selected campaign.

![](/images/imported/How-to-find-customers-who-interacted-with-a-campaign-(11).png)

- Find customers who clicked on a link in the campaign.

![](/images/imported/How-to-find-customers-who-interacted-with-a-campaign-(12).png)

- Find customers who did not click in the campaign.

![](/images/imported/How-to-find-customers-who-interacted-with-a-campaign-(24).png)

- Find customers who clicked the unsubscribe link.

![](/images/imported/How-to-find-customers-who-interacted-with-a-campaign-(14).png)

- Find all campaign participants — all customers who were included in the campaign’s recipient list.

![](/images/imported/--2026-01-08--15_39_58.png)

## Searching by interaction time

- Find customers who opened the campaign in June 2024.

![](/images/imported/How-to-find-customers-who-interacted-with-a-campaign-(16).png)

- Find customers who opened the campaign within the last hour.

![](/images/imported/How-to-find-customers-who-interacted-with-a-campaign-(17).png)

## Searching by number of interactions

- Find customers who clicked in the campaign two or more times.

![](/images/imported/How-to-find-customers-who-interacted-with-a-campaign-(18).png)

- Find customers who were sent the campaign three times in the last six months.

![](/images/imported/How-to-find-customers-who-interacted-with-a-campaign-(19).png)

# How to find specific Campaign engagements (e.g. clicked, sent, opened etc.)

Go to **Customer Data Platform → Campaign engagements**.

![](/images/imported/How-to-find-customers-who-interacted-with-a-campaign-(20).png)

- Find campaign sends.

![](/images/imported/How-to-find-customers-who-interacted-with-a-campaign-(21).png)

- Find campaign opens.

![](/images/imported/How-to-find-customers-who-interacted-with-a-campaign-(22).png)

- Find campaign clicks in the last 24 hours. 

![](/images/imported/How-to-find-customers-who-interacted-with-a-campaign-(23).png)
