title: "Maestra AI"
---

**Maestra AI** is a built-in analytics assistant that uses machine learning to analyze your business and marketing data, highlight growth opportunities, and help you make data-driven decisions.

Maestra AI processes aggregated, anonymized metrics from your project — it does not share any information that directly identifies your company, including its name.

# How it works

Maestra AI connects to external machine learning services to analyze the data collected within your project. The insights it generates are based on four key data categories: business metrics, flows, campaigns, and subscribers.

The up-to-date list of machine learning services powering Maestra AI includes: [OpenAI](https://openai.com/policies/), [Anthropic](https://privacy.anthropic.com/en/collections/10663362-consumers), [Google Gemini](https://support.google.com/gemini/answer/13594961), and [OpenRouter](https://openrouter.ai/privacy).

<Warning>
The services used and the data processed are subject to change without additional notice. New services may be added or existing ones removed. The most up-to-date information about machine learning services and the data shared with them can always be found on the **Maestra AI** settings page.
</Warning>

# How to enable Maestra AI

1. Go to **Settings** → **System** → **Maestra AI**.
  ![](/images/maestra-ai/maestra-ai-overview.png)
2. Review the list of machine learning services and the data categories being processed.
3. Click **Agree and enable** to activate Maestra AI. By doing so, you agree to the terms and policies of all listed services, including the terms of data processing.

# Data being processed

The Maestra AI settings page lists all data categories that are shared with machine learning services. You can expand each category to see exactly what data is included.

## Business Metrics

This category covers the core performance indicators of your business.
![](/images/maestra-ai/business-metrics-expanded.png)

The following data is processed:

- **Business Metrics**: Total Revenue, Orders, Average Order Value, Purchase Frequency, First Purchase Rate, Repeat Purchase Rate.
- **Campaigns Metrics**: Campaigns Revenue, Orders from Campaigns, Campaigns Revenue Share, Average Revenue per Recipient, Average Order Value from Campaigns, Subscribers, Average Delivered Campaigns per Recipient.
- **Website Personalization metrics**: Revenue/Orders from personalization, revenue share, and average order value from personalization mechanics.
- **In-App Metrics**: Revenue/Orders from In-App, revenue share, and average order value from In-App campaigns.
- Total Customers on a specific date.
- New Customer Profiles for the period.
- Brand industry and lifecycle segment boundaries.
- Number of customers in each lifecycle segment.

## Flows

This category includes data related to your automated flows.
![](/images/maestra-ai/flows-expanded.png)

The following data is processed:

- All flow names and their IDs.
- Number of flow runs.
- All campaign metrics from flows, broken down by channels and campaigns.

## Campaigns

This category includes data about your marketing campaigns across all channels.
![](/images/maestra-ai/campaigns-expanded.png)

The following data is processed:

- Campaign names and IDs.
- Campaign type (automatic, transactional or bulk).
- Campaign channel.
- All metrics: Sends, deliveries, opens, clicks, attributed revenue and orders, subscriptions and unsubscriptions.

## Subscribers

This category covers subscription-related data.
![](/images/maestra-ai/subscribers-expanded.png)

The following data is processed:

- Unsubscriptions and subscriptions for the period.
- Number of subscribed and unavailable customers on a specific date.

# Privacy and data security

Maestra AI is designed with privacy in mind. The key principles are:

- **No company identification**: information that directly identifies your company (including its name) is never shared with external services.
- **Aggregated data only**: the system processes aggregated metrics and statistics, not individual customer records.
- **Transparent service list**: the full list of machine learning services and their privacy policies is always available on the settings page.
- **Opt-in activation**: Maestra AI is not enabled by default — you must explicitly agree to the terms and enable it.
