---
title: "How to Filter Customers into Segments"
---

To create a segment:

1. Go to **Customer Data Platform** → **Customers**:

![](/images/imported/--2025-07-30--3_28_53PM.png)

2. Build a filter to select the customers you want to add into your segment and click **Apply**:

![](/images/imported/--2025-07-30--3_35_44PM.png)

3. Click **Create segment**
​

![](/images/imported/--2025-07-30--3_33_17PM.png)

4. Enter the segment name and description. The system will automatically generate the system name and external ID.
​
5. You can create a **Real-time** or a **Scheduled segment,** depending on your needs.
​[Types of customer segments](/segments/types-of-customer-segments) article can help you choose the right type of segment for your needs.

- To create a **Scheduled segment**, tick the **Set an update schedule** checkbox and configure the update rules based on time and specific events (if needed). Then click **Create**.

![](/images/imported/--2025-07-30--3_39_43PM.png)

- To create a **Real-time segment** just click **Create**:

![](/images/imported/--2025-07-30--3_40_57PM.png)
