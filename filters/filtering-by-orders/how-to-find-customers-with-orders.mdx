---
title: "How to find customers with orders"
---

To find customers with orders, go to the **Customer Data Platform → Customers**.

![](/images/imported/Find-customers-with-orders.png)

- Find customers with an order in a specific status.  In the screenshot, we’re filtering orders with any of the following statuses: Placed / Paid / Delivered.  You can apply the same logic or specify a particular status if needed.
  ![](/images/imported/Find-customers-with-orders-(1).png)

<Note>
Please note that the filter is based on the **order’s latest status**. For example, if an order goes through several statuses — Placed → Paid → Delivered — it will be considered as Delivered in the filter.If an order goes from Placed → Paid → Canceled, it will be considered as Canceled in the filter.
</Note>

- Find customers who purchased a specific product with the order status Placed, Paid, or Delivered
  ![](/images/imported/Find-customers-with-orders-(2).png)
- Find customers who placed an order with the order status Placed, Paid, or Delivered in the last 6 months.
  ![](/images/imported/Find-customers-with-orders-(3).png)
- Find customers with two or more orders with the order status Placed, Paid, or Delivered
  ![](/images/imported/Find-customers-with-orders-(4).png)
- Find customers with order total spend of 200$ or more. 
  ![](/images/imported/Find-customers-with-orders-(5).png)
There's no need to specify statuses. Cancelled and returned items are not included in the total order value.
- Find customers with orders from a specific touchpoint with the order status Placed, Paid, or Delivered.
  ![](/images/imported/Find-customers-with-orders--286-29.png)
- Find customers with orders from a specific brand with the order status Placed, Paid, or Delivered
  ![](/images/imported/Find-customers-with-orders-(7).png)
