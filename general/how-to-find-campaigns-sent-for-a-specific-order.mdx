---
title: "How to Find Campaigns Sent for a Specific Order"
---

**Use case:** find campaigns sent for a specific order, such as payment or delivery notifications.

Go to **Customer Data Platform → Campaign engagements** and set up the filter.

![](/images/imported/How-to-find-campaigns-related-to-an-order.png)

Add a condition to find **sent campaigns only**. 
​

![](/images/imported/How-to-find-campaigns-related-to-an-order-(1).png)

## Which campaigns are included in the filter

For a campaign to appear in the filter, the following conditions must be met:

- The flow that sends the campaign must be triggered by the order event.

![](/images/imported/--2026-02-13--18_04_28.png)

- The method that sends/updates the order must use one of the events shown in the screenshot.

![](/images/imported/How-to-find-orders-with-a-specific-value-(18).png)

All steps are supported except the **Get order list** step.
