---
title: "How to find customers with send failures or undeliveries"
---

Customers may not receive a campaign if:

- **Failed to send from Maestra** (e.g., no subscription, expired consent, customer in Control Groups list, etc.);
- **Failed to deliver to the recipient's email server**.

Both types of events can be found in **campaign engagement tab**.
​

![](/images/imported/--2026-01-15--17_46_09.png)

You can also view the **error reason** and additional details there.

![](/images/imported/--2026-01-15--17_49_46.png)

Campaign engagements and customers can be **filtered by these reasons**.

- Find all undeliveries with the reason **Mailbox full**.

![](/images/imported/How-to-find-clicks-on-specific-links-in-campaigns-(8).png)

- Find all send failures where the message could not be composed.

![](/images/imported/How-to-find-customers-with-send-failures-or-undeliveries.png)

- Find customers who had two or more undeliveries with the reason **Recipient rejected email due to content** for the last week.

![](/images/imported/How-to-find-customers-with-send-failures-or-undeliveries-(1).png)
