---
title: "How to find clicks on specific links in campaigns"
---

You can use filters **to find customers** who clicked on specific links within campaigns.

To do this, select the following filter: **Campaign clicked → Link click → Link**

![](/images/imported/How-to-find-clicks-on-specific-links-in-campaigns.png)

**Note:** The filter does not support links containing `?`, anchors (`#abc`), or variables.

For example, clicks on the link below cannot be found by entering the full URL in the search.
​`https://demoshop.maestra.cloud/?utm_source=maestra&utm_medium=email&utm_campaign=Bulk` 

![](/images/imported/How-to-find-clicks-on-specific-links-in-campaigns-(1).png)

In the filter, you should only enter the part of the URL **before the `?`**.

![](/images/imported/How-to-find-clicks-on-specific-links-in-campaigns-(2).png)

This type of filter will find clicks on the specified link **with any variables**, including `click id` and UTM tags.

It is not necessary to enter the full URL. **Partial URL search** is supported.

![](/images/imported/How-to-find-clicks-on-specific-links-in-campaigns-(3).png)

You can also specify **which campaigns’ clicks** you are interested in,

![](/images/imported/How-to-find-clicks-on-specific-links-in-campaigns-(4).png)

and specify the **time period** for the clicks.

![](/images/imported/How-to-find-clicks-on-specific-links-in-campaigns-(5).png)

To see all **click actions** across all users, build the filter on the **Customer Data Platform → Campaign engagements **tab.

![](/images/imported/How-to-find-clicks-on-specific-links-in-campaigns-(6).png)
