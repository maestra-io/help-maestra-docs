---
title: "Who is a Customer?"
---

A customer in Maestra is any consumer that can be identified and found in the system.\
\
Once recorded in a project, every customer must have at least one **unique contact detail / ID,** such as:

- email;
- mobile phone number;
- discount card number;
- external ID (an ID issued by your external system; you’ll need to create an additional [custom field](/api-integrations/how-to-create-a-custom-field) to save this info (field type: Unique ID, entity: Customer);
- mobile app;
- mobile push device.

In addition, a Maestra ID is generated automatically for all customers recorded in the project.

The system also creates an individual customer profile for every customer.

# Navigating the customer profile

![02\_whois](/images/imported/02_whois.png)

In the center are the main contacts and personal information of a customer: email, mobile phone number, birthdate, gender, area and time zone.

Below, you'll find IDs and discount cards, along with a detailed history of past customer identifiers (**ID Changelog**) if they’ve been updated. To the right of this section, there’s an authentication token. Clicking on it copies the customer's authorization token value directly to the clipboard.

In the right area — devices.

# Actions with customer profile

Customer profiles allow you to:

- edit customer details;
- change subscriptions;
- issue actions.

Customers can be added to the database:

- [manually](/customers-orders-and-products/customers/how-to-add-a-customer-manually-via-the-user-interface), one at a time;
- [via file import](/customers-orders-and-products/customers/how-to-bulk-import-customers-from-a-file);
- by importing a customer from an FTP server;
- via [API](https://developers.maestra.io/docs/bulk-import-of-customers).
