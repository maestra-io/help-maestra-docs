---
title: "What is an Action?"
---

**An action** is any event or change  **related to a customer**.

Every change in purchase status, participation in a campaign, product view, or edit to a customer profile is recorded as an action in the customer profile.

# **What an action includes**

Each action includes:

- **Action template** (what happened)
- **Date** (when it happened)
- **Touchpoint** (where it happened)
- **Brand** (for multi-brand projects)

## Action template

An action template is the name of the event — it describes what happened.

All templates are divided into two top-level categories:

**1. Personal actions**

Actions performed by the customer, for example:

- viewing a product
- opening a campaign
- placing an order

**2. Actions performed for a customer**

Actions initiated by your team, for example:

- correcting errors
- sending a campaign
- issuing a reward

Action templates can also be grouped using tags.

See the [guide on action templates](/customers-orders-and-products/actions/how-to-create-an-action-template) for more details on how to create them.

## Date

The date shows when the action happened.

Sometimes actions are added with a delay, or historical data is uploaded to the project. In these cases, two timestamps are stored:

- the time of the event
- the time when the event was recorded in Maestra

## Touchpoint

A touchpoint shows the channel or place where the action happened (for example, a website, store, or social network). Touchpoints are grouped into channels.

## Brand

For multi-brand projects only, the brand field shows which brand the action belongs to.

## **Where an action is stored**

All customer actions are stored in the **Сustomer profile** on the **Activity Feed → All activity**.

![](/images/imported/What-is-an-Action_-(2).png)

You can also **view customer activity on the actions page.**

## **Where an action is used**

- **Orders** — every time an order status changes, it’s recorded as a separate action (created, purchased, paid, delivered, returned).
- **[Campaigns](https://help.maestra.io/en/collections/14324580-campaigns)** — changes in campaign status for each customer (sent, opened, clicked) are recorded as actions in the customer profile.
- **Promo codes** — issuing a promo code is also recorded as an action.
- **Customer profile** — edits to personal data or subscriptions are recorded as actions.
- **Customer notes and tags**, and other customer-related events.
